# Supported Networks

## EVM Networks

- Ethereum
- Avalanche
- Fantom
- Polygon
- Moonbeam

EVM networks can be the destination of token transfers and contract calls.



## Non-EVM Networks 

- Axelarnet
- cosmoshub
- terra
- crescent
- juno
- osmosis
- osmosis-2

Non-EVM networks can only be the destination of token transfers.
